Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Warranty Manager ("we", "our", "app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Phone Number: Used for OTP authentication via Firebase.
  • Email Address: Used for sending warranty reminder alerts (optional).
  • Device Information: Device name, category, purchase date, warranty details.
  • Images: Bill photos uploaded by the user (optional).
  • Profile Information: Name, mobile number, and email (optional).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used only for the following purposes:

  • To provide warranty tracking functionality
  • To send notifications and reminders
  • To authenticate users via OTP (Firebase Authentication)
  • To store and manage your devices locally

3. Data Storage

  • All device and profile data is stored locally on your device.
  • Images (bill photos) are stored in your device’s internal storage.
  • Authentication is handled securely via Firebase.

We do not store your personal data on external servers (except Firebase authentication).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

We only use trusted services such as:

  • Google Firebase (for OTP authentication)

5. Notifications

The app may send notifications about warranty expiry dates. You can enable or disable notifications within the app.

6. Email Alerts

If you provide your email address, the app may send warranty reminder emails. This feature is optional and can be disabled.

7. Data Deletion

You have full control over your data.

  • You can delete individual devices anytime.
  • You can delete your entire account and all associated data directly from the app.

To delete your account:

  1. Go to Account section
  2. Tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm deletion

All stored data including devices, profile, and settings will be permanently removed.

8. Security

We implement security measures such as:

  • PIN-based authentication
  • Encrypted PIN storage (hashed)
  • Secure Firebase authentication

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
