Warranty Manager

Android Application by Suresh

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Warranty Manager collects the following information:

  • Your name (first and last) provided during registration
  • Your email address obtained via Google Sign-in
  • Device details including name, brand, category, purchase date, and warranty period
  • Bill and receipt photos you choose to upload (optional)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Display your warranty information within the app
  • Send you warranty expiry push notifications
  • Send email reminders before your warranty expires

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal information or device details to any external server without your consent.

4. Email Alerts

If you provide your email address, we will use it only to send warranty expiry alerts. We will never share your email with third parties or use it for marketing purposes.

5. Photos and Attachments

Bill photos you attach are stored locally on your device only. They are not uploaded to any server.

6. Google Sign-in

We use Google Sign-in (by Google) to verify your identity during registration. Google may process your name and email address as part of this authentication. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.

7. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your phone/mobile number
  • We do NOT collect payment information
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT show advertisements
  • We do NOT share data with third parties

8. Your Rights

You may delete your account and all associated data at any time using the Delete Account option in the app Account screen. All locally stored data will be permanently removed immediately.

9. Security

  • PIN-based authentication for daily app access
  • PIN stored as SHA-256 hash — never as plain text
  • Secure Google authentication

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: dakshin.appdev.corp@gmail.com

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
